Instructions

  1. Seasoned the fish strips. Coat the fish with fish batter. Remove excess batter.
  2. Deep fry for 2 mins. Dry. Set aside. Repeat again after 2 mins. Place paper towel to oil dry
  3. Mix all ingredients for the dip and place in a sealed glass container. Better make ahead for best tasting.
